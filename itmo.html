Mathematics Olympiad

I am a fellow mathematics lover not a nerdy course book one, but more like solving questions with your IQ and logics. I got selected for International Teenager's Mathematics Olympiad in 2019. It was a fun event where I met different kinds of people from all around the world and made friends around the globe.

The event took place in CMS, Gomti nagar-1, Lucknow, India. All the participants along with their teachers reached there at 13th October 2019. We had a heart welcoming program to start the event by the talented kids of CMS. We even had wonderful, varieties of Indian dishes that would leave all the plates shining at the end. The examination day was quite interesting, after we all participants were finished with our examination we made different groups and presented our logics on the given questions. We even roamed around the beautiful mesmerizing city that didn't failed to take our hearts away.

I really had an amazing moment out there meeeting all kinds of people, eating the authentic food, dancing, singing, and many more. This event wasn't just c competition insted it was a journey to put our hearts together in one place happily.
